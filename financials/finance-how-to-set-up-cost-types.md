## <a name="to-transfer-the-general-ledger-chart-of-accounts-to-the-chart-of-cost-types"></a><span data-ttu-id="46d39-111">Para transferir el plan de cuentas de contabilidad al plan de tipos de coste</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="46d39-111">To transfer the general ledger chart of accounts to the chart of cost types</span></span>

## <a name="to-update-cost-types"></a><span data-ttu-id="46d39-132">Para actualizar tipos de coste</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="46d39-132">To update cost types</span></span>
