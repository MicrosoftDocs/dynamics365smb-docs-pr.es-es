## <a name="to-correct-a-posted-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="4984c-113">Para corregir una factura de compra registrada</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4984c-113">To correct a posted purchase invoice</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="4984c-114">Elija el icono ![Bombilla que abre la función Dígame](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Dígame qué desea hacer"), escriba **Histórico facturas compra** y luego elija el enlace relacionado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4984c-114">Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Posted Purchase Invoices**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]

    <span data-ttu-id="4984c-121">Un abono de compra se crea y se registra automáticamente para anular la factura de compra registrada inicialmente.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4984c-121">A purchase credit memo is automatically created and posted to void the initial posted purchase invoice.</span></span>
