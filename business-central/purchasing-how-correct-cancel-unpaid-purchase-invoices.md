## <a name="to-correct-a-posted-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="e4c83-114">Para corregir una factura de compra registrada</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e4c83-114">To correct a posted purchase invoice</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
