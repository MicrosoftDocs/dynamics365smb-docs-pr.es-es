## <a name="to-prioritize-vendors"></a><span data-ttu-id="1aa37-107">Para dar prioridad a proveedores</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa37-107">To prioritize vendors</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="1aa37-108">Elija el icono ![Bombilla que abre la función Dígame](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Dígame qué desea hacer"), escriba **Proveedores** y luego elija el enlace relacionado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="1aa37-108">Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Vendors**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
